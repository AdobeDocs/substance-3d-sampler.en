# Material presets

![alt text](../../assets/6.0_materialPresets.png)

When you add a new material from the **[Project panel](project-panel.md)**, the **Create new material** window appears. From here you can select presets to quickly configure the channels for your new material.

Presets are available for both OpenPBR and Adobe Standard Material models, and the preset channels will change based on which material model is selected.

You can customize the channels for your material with the **Edit list** button. This will bring you to the [**Channel selection window**](channel-settings-panel.md) where you can update the list of channels for your new material, or add new presets.
