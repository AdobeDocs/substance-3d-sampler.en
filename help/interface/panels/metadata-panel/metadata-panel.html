Metadata panel

In the metadata panel, you can access and modify the metadata of your asset.

Name: The name of the asset.

Description: Description of your asset or description embedded in the Substance material

Category: Category of your asset or category embedded in the Substance material

Author: Author of your asset or author embedded in the Substance material. By default, the author's name is the name of your OS account.

Creation date: Creation date of your asset in Sampler or the date of import to Sampler. (This cannot be edited)

Update date: The last date your asset was updated. (This cannot be edited)

Tags: Tags of your asset or tags embedded in the Substance material.

Physical size: X, Y, and Z size of your asset.

Rating: Rating of your asset.

Custom Metadata

All custom metadata will be included in the material file (SBSAR) to ensure a more efficient workflow for sharing digital materials across applications.

New text field (add a custom field for metadata):

  1. Insert name. The name of a custom metadata must be a valid XML element name (i.e. : no spaces or special characters, does not start with a number...)
  2. Insert description/note

Modify custom metadata fields by hovering your cursor over the field and selecting either the edit or delete button that appears.
