# Generators

Generators are filters that make an additive change to your material. Generators often result in a substantial change to the appearance of your material, and are regularly used to create the foundations of a material as a result.

There are two main forms of generator:

* Converting generators convert the underlying material into a new form such as using the **Panel filter** to convert a flat steel material into multiple panels.
* Adding generators add new elements on top of the underlying material, such as the **Atlas Scatter filter** that scatters objects across a material from an input atlas.
