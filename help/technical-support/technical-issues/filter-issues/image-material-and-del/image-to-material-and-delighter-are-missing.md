# Image to Material and Delighter are missing

The **Image to Material (AI Powered)** and **Delighter** can be missing from the filter list if your system doesn't meet the [System requirements](https://substance3d.adobe.com/documentation/display/DRAFTSAMPLER/.System+requirements+vDraftVersion).
